## Product Requirements Document

### 1. Introduction

- **Product Name:** picoQUEUE
- **Product Type:** Mobile Application (read-it-later)
- **Target User:** Lifelong Learners
- **Goal:** To help users develop a consistent reading habit and make progress on their learning goals by providing a curated reading list experience.

### 2. Goals

- Enable users to save and organize interesting articles and content in one place.
- Help users prioritize their reading list and focus on the most important content.
- Encourage users to develop a consistent reading habit by providing a to-do list experience.
- Provide a curated reading experience.

### 3. Target Audience

- Lifelong Learners: Individuals passionate about learning and personal growth.
- Professionals: Individuals who need to stay up-to-date on industry news and trends.

### 4. Features

- **Core Features:**
  - Save Content: Save articles, videos, and other web content to the user's queue.
  - To-Do List/Task Management: Manage saved content as a to-do list, marking items as 'completed'.
  - Content Prioritization: Prioritize content within their queue.
- **Additional Features (Future Enhancements):**
  - Reading Time Estimation: Estimate the reading time for each saved article.
  - Content Filtering/Tagging: Filter and tag content for easier organization.
  - Cross-Device Sync: Synchronize the user's queue and reading progress across multiple devices.

### 5. Use Cases

- A user saves an article they find interesting but don't have time to read immediately.
- The user adds the saved article to their reading list and prioritizes it based on importance.
- The user sets a goal to read at least one article from their reading list each day.
- The user marks articles as 'completed' as they read them, tracking their progress.

### 6. Release Criteria

- The application must be able to save content from various sources.
- The application must provide a to-do list interface for managing saved content.
- The application must allow users to prioritize content within their queue.
- The application must have a clean and intuitive user interface.

### 7. Future Considerations

- Content recommendation engine to suggest high-quality content.
- Integration with habit tracking apps.
- Social sharing features.
